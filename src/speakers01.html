Aloysius Yapp

Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman, Assistant Professor

Dr. Aloysius Yapp is a leading expert in the field of immersive technology and its applications in education and cultural heritage preservation. As an Assistant Professor at Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman (UTAR), he has made significant contributions to the Department of Game Studies, particularly in the Centre for Immersive Technology and Creativity, which he chaired from 2013 to 2023.

Dr. Yapp's research focuses on leveraging virtual and augmented reality to enhance learning experiences and preserve cultural heritage. His recent work has delved into integrating AI technology to create immersive metaverse experiences. He has published extensively and delivered numerous lectures on topics such as immersive technology, mixed media, animation, and graphic novels.

Beyond his academic pursuits, Dr. Yapp has served in various administrative roles at UTAR, including Deputy Dean for Student Development and Industrial Training. He has also been involved in industry collaborations as a consultant and has contributed to developing educational programs as an external examiner and academic advisor. His commitment to advancing immersive technology and its impact on creative industries is evident in his diverse professional experiences and ongoing research.
